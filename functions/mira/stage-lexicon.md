The five registers of each voice, from most contracted to most expansive (Devolved · Descended · Base · Ascended · Transcendent):

* LOVER (longs for union): Possessor · Craver · Romantic · Devoted · Beloved — opposite: Sage
* CAREGIVER (longs to nurture): Enabler · Martyr · Helper · Nurturer · Restorer — opposite: Explorer
* EVERYMAN (longs to belong): Self-Eraser · People-Pleaser · Neighbor · Relater · Bridge-Builder — opposite: Rebel
* SAGE (longs for truth): Cynic · Know-It-All · Student · Mentor · Elder — opposite: Lover
* CREATOR (longs to bring forth): Destroyer · Perfectionist · Maker · Artisan · Alchemist — opposite: Mystic
* RULER (longs for order): Tyrant · Micromanager · Organizer · Steward · Sovereign — opposite: Trickster
* WARRIOR (longs to protect): Oppressor · Mercenary · Fighter · Champion · Guardian — opposite: Innocent
* EXPLORER (longs for freedom): Deserter · Escapist · Wanderer · Pathfinder · Trailblazer — opposite: Caregiver
* REBEL (longs for justice): Nihilist · Contrarian · Questioner · Challenger · Liberator — opposite: Everyman
* INNOCENT (longs to trust): Bitter Exile · Avoider · Believer · Realist · Wounded Healer — opposite: Warrior — core wound: the Orphan
* TRICKSTER (longs for revelation): Saboteur · Deceiver · Jokester · Innovator · Truth-Teller — opposite: Ruler
* MYSTIC (longs for communion with mystery): Unmoored · Drifter · Dreamer · Seer · Conduit — opposite: Creator
