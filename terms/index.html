TERMS

The understanding between us

A few honest terms for using The Art of Soulcraft. No fine print you need a lawyer to decode — just what this is, what it isn't, and what you can expect.

Last updated July 2026

What this is for

The Art of Soulcraft is a tool for personal reflection and growth. It's meant to help you look at yourself with more honesty and more compassion — nothing more, and nothing less.

What this is not

This is not clinical, and not a substitute for professional mental health support. It doesn't diagnose, treat, or assess any condition. If you're struggling, please reach out to a qualified professional — a therapist, counselor, or physician. This tool can sit alongside that work, but it can never replace it.

Your results are a map, not a diagnosis. A map shows you terrain and possible paths; it doesn't tell you who you are or predict where you'll end up. Hold your reading lightly, as a mirror to think with — not a verdict.

Changes to the system

This is a living project. We reserve the right to update the assessment, the writing, the features, and the pricing as it grows. We'll try to do so thoughtfully, but the system you use today may look different tomorrow.

Payments and refunds

The paid tiers are digital products delivered instantly. Because of that, we don't offer refunds once a reading has been accessed — there's no way to un-see it. If something genuinely went wrong on our end, write to us and we'll make it right.

Age

You must be at least 13 years old to use The Art of Soulcraft.

Whose work this is

The assessment, the writing, the archetype system, and the design are the intellectual property of BridgeTender Studio. You're welcome to share your own results and talk about your experience; please don't copy or reproduce the underlying system as your own.

Questions

Anything you'd like clarified? Write to hello@artofsoulcraft.com — we're glad to talk.
