Five Stages of Bandwidth

Every archetype exists on one spectrum — from all your capacity spent holding yourself together, to capacity overflowing outward to others. Descent narrows; ascent integrates. Read the full explainer →

Devolved

Bandwidth near empty — the energy captured by ego: domination, consumption, control.

Descended

The energy bent by fear: grasping, pettiness, defense.

Base

The everyday, unexamined expression — where the day begins.

Ascended

The energy mastered and matured.

Transcendent

Bandwidth overflowing — the energy given away, freely available to others and the whole.

The Four Temperaments

Not how resourced you are, but how you show up — the faculty you meet a moment through, before you've decided to give anything at all. Read the full explainer →

Soul

Pairings

What emerges when two archetypes combine — both stay present and distinct; nothing dissolves into a third thing. Your top two voices name a Pairing: the Creator and the Mystic together read as The Magician; the Explorer and the Creator, The Cartographer.

There are 66 in all — one for every pair of the twelve. Your results name the three that arise from your top three voices.
